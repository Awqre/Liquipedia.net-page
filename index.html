How To Contribute

Learn how to contribute to the wiki

Contributing to the wiki is actually pretty easy. Most people start by fixing typos, which is actually the easiest way to contribute. You just have to log in, click edit, find and fix the typo, click save, and you are done.
Another way many contributors start with is keeping brackets up to date during tournaments. Most of the times the pages are already set up by one of the more experienced contributors, and people just have to fill them. Filling a bracket is pretty straightforward. You log in, click on edit, find the bracket and update scores and fill in names. If you are unsure, just look how it is done on other pages. Looking how things are done on other pages gives you a good idea of how you can do it yourself.
If you feel comfortable with wiki editing or if you want to learn the advanced stuff, feel free to browse our "How to contribute" sections you can find in the menus on the left of the wiki pages. You can find us in our IRC channel #liquipedia on QuakeNet.
